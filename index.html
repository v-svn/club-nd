Who are we?

Founded in January 2026, Sovereign is a drifting club in MTA Drift Paradise that began with a simple moment—a tandem drift between XXDyy and rYong in Sadamine Touge. From that mountain road, a desire arose to build something bigger than just drifting, a place for drifters to gather and grow together.

Sovereign exists to unite players passionate about drifting, while also providing opportunities for anyone looking to learn, gain experience, and make new friends. For us, drifting isn't just about technique; it's also about connection, teamwork, and the excitement that comes with every turn.

Carrying the Japanese drift style identity, Sovereign is inspired by JDM culture, stance cars, and the iconic touge feel. Each car and drifting style reflects the character of each member, making Sovereign not just a club, but a community with a strong style and character.
